About BLOSK

BLOSK is a next generation platform where creators can shape anything, make anything using presets, and design interactive worlds for players. Users can explore and play these worlds, while creators have complete control over their environment.

Future updates will include collaboration features that allow multiple creators to work together and support for uploading custom models, expanding what is possible on the platform. BLOSK is a space where imagination meets capability and creativity drives the experience.

For Creators

Full Creative Control

Creators can design every aspect of their world using presets and advanced tools. Shape landscapes, objects, and gameplay systems to build unique experiences for players.

Advanced Tools

Drag and drop presets, BLOSK gives creators the power to bring their vision to life without limits (For the most part lol).

Collaboration

Collaboration features are coming soon, allowing multiple creators to work together, share assets, and co-create larger worlds.

Future Features

Upcoming updates will include the ability to upload custom models and further expand creative possibilities. BLOSK is continually evolving to give creators more freedom and flexibility.

For Players

Playable Worlds

Players can explore immersive worlds made by creators. Every environment is interactive and designed to provide engaging experiences.

Community Interaction

Users can provide feedback, participate in events, and support creators as they develop new content. While editing worlds will not be allowed by users, users can enjoy rich and interactive gameplay experiences.

Future Engagement

Future updates will allow players to participate in collaborative projects and experience worlds with enhanced interactivity through shared contributions.

Community

BLOSK is a growing network of creators, developers, and players. Users can explore worlds, provide feedback, and support creators as they build new experiences.

We host events, challenges, and showcases to highlight top creations and encourage creativity. The community is inclusive, supportive, and dedicated to pushing the boundaries of interactive experiences.

Creator Tools

The BLOSK toolkit includes:

  • Drag and drop presets for quick creation
  • Asset library and marketplace for sharing resources
  • Future multiplayer collaboration tools for co-creation
  • Real-time testing and editing environment
