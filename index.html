About BLOSK

BLOSK is a next generation platform where creators can shape anything, make anything using presets, and design interactive worlds for players. Users can explore and play these worlds, while creators have complete control over their environment.

Future updates will include collaboration features that allow multiple creators to work together and support for uploading custom models, expanding what is possible on the platform. BLOSK is a space where imagination meets capability and creativity drives the experience.

Key Features

Creator Control

Creators can design every part of their world using presets and tools provided. They can shape landscapes, objects, and gameplay systems to create unique experiences for players.

Playable Worlds

Players can explore and enjoy worlds made by creators. While editing is restricted for regular users, they can experience fully interactive environments designed by the community.

Advanced Tools

From simple drag and drop presets to more complex scripting options, BLOSK gives creators the power to build worlds that match their vision.

Community Collaboration

Collaboration features are planned for the future. Creators will be able to work together on projects, share assets, and co-create larger experiences.

Future Features

Upcoming updates will include the ability to upload custom models and further expand the capabilities of your worlds. BLOSK is continually evolving to give creators more freedom.

Our Community

BLOSK is more than a platform. It is a growing network of creators, developers, and players. Users can explore worlds, provide feedback, and support creators as they build new experiences.

We host events, challenges, and showcases to highlight top creations and encourage creativity. The community is inclusive, supportive, and dedicated to pushing the boundaries of interactive experiences.

Creator Tools

The BLOSK toolkit includes:

  • Drag and drop presets for quick creation
  • Advanced scripting system for customizing gameplay
  • Asset library and marketplace for sharing resources
  • Future multiplayer collaboration tools for co creation
  • Real time testing and editing environment

Join BLOSK

We invite creators and early contributors to help shape BLOSK. Whether you design, build, or play, BLOSK offers a space to explore, create, and share immersive experiences.
