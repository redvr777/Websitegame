Welcome to BLOSK

We’re starting a new project called BLOSK. Think of it like Roblox—but almost everything in the world is editable by users. You can tweak, build, and create freely.

What you can do:

  • Edit nearly every part of the world
  • Create and share your own games and experiences
  • Use simple but powerful tools for building
  • Collaborate and share with others in the community
  • Get early access if you’re invited

Want to join the early discussion? Check out our Discord.
