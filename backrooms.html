You are in the Backrooms...

This is currently just an idea — not a real game yet. But if it comes to life, it will be a hyper-realistic multiplayer experience where you and your friends wander through endless, buzzing fluorescent hallways.

The corridors twist and repeat endlessly, the lights flicker unpredictably, and faint, unplaceable noises echo from unseen rooms. Every corner hides subtle anomalies — warped walls, strange furniture, and doors that lead nowhere.

Survive, explore, and uncover secrets — if you dare.
